# Business Requirements Document: Statman

## Business objective

Build a community-first sports profile platform where athletes want to maintain beautiful public profiles, fans can discover fresh local sports content, and teams can support profile accuracy through fast stat capture.

## Business positioning

Statman is not just a stat tracker. It is an athlete exposure platform with stat credibility, media, and social momentum.

## Initial business strategy

- Prioritize community growth before monetization.
- Keep MVP free.
- Build habit around game-day stat updates and share cards.
- Use public profiles as acquisition loops.
- Let seeded/imported profiles create browseable supply before claim.

## Future monetization paths

- Freemium public profiles.
- Paid team tools.
- Advanced stat entry/import tools.
- Recruiting profile tier.
- Media/profile customization upgrades.
- School, league, or club packages.
- Data/API products later.

## Key business assumptions

- Athletes will update profiles if the result looks good and gives exposure.
- Coaches/managers will support stats if the UI is faster than spreadsheets.
- Fans will follow athlete and team updates if the feed feels fresh and credible.
- Public sourced data can seed initial profiles and discovery.
- Verification and dispute features are required to avoid trust collapse.

## Primary value propositions

### Athlete

- Better public profile.
- Faster updates.
- Shareable stat moments.
- Exposure and confidence.

### Fan/sports lover

- Fresh athlete and game content.
- Easy follows.
- Live/spectator game views.
- Rankings and discovery.

### Team manager

- Faster stat capture.
- Bulk roster/stat entry.
- Team-controlled verification.
- Better athlete promotion.

## Risks

- Empty network at launch.
- Too much sport complexity too early.
- Verification disputes becoming operationally heavy.
- Scraped/imported data quality issues.
- Mobile UX becoming too dashboard-like.
- YAML/API contracts becoming unmaintainable.

## Business milestone targets

### Demo

- 20 athlete profiles.
- 2 teams.
- 1 league.
- 3 completed games.
- Live capture demo.
- Share cards.

### Private beta

- 5 to 10 teams.
- 100 to 200 athlete profiles.
- Basic follower/reaction loop.
- Team manager stat entry validated.

### Public beta

- Public profile claiming.
- Personalized/localized homepage.
- Explore rankings.
- Import/source tracking.
- Dispute workflow.
